# Customer Loyalty & Product Insights Dashboard | Power BI

## Project Summary

The Customer Loyalty & Product Insights Dashboard is a Business Intelligence solution developed in Power BI to analyze customer loyalty trends, regional customer distribution, and product performance. The dashboard converts raw customer and product data into meaningful insights that support data-driven decision-making and customer retention strategies.

This project demonstrates practical experience in data transformation, data modeling, business analysis, and dashboard development using Power BI.

---

## Business Objective

The objective of this project is to provide stakeholders with a centralized view of customer loyalty performance and product ratings to better understand customer behavior and identify opportunities for business growth.

### Key Questions Addressed

* Which loyalty tier has the highest customer participation?
* How are loyal customers distributed across regions?
* Which brands receive the highest customer ratings?
* What is the overall loyalty program composition?
* How can customer and product insights support business decisions?

## Key Findings

### Customer Insights

* Gold members represent the largest share of the customer base.
* North America demonstrates higher customer loyalty participation than Europe.
* Premium and Standard customer segments contribute significantly to overall loyalty membership.

### Product Insights

* SoftWear achieved the highest customer rating.
* AquaPro and CapCo also demonstrated strong performance.
* WarmGear received the lowest rating and has been discontinued.

### Business Insights

* Customer loyalty programs show strong adoption among higher-value customer segments.
* High-rated brands contribute positively to customer satisfaction.
* Regional loyalty trends provide opportunities for targeted customer engagement strategies.
